### Hi, I'm Trung 👋

I began my journey into the world of code at the age of 12. For a long time, I thought what drove me was a love for programming itself. But when I looked closer and asked what truly kept pulling me back, I realized it wasn’t just the code - it was curiosity.

I’ve always been curious about how things are built, how systems work, and how the world fits together. Today, I’m a software engineer, but I don’t let that title define me. At heart, I’m a lifelong explorer, driven by the need to understand the “how” and “why” behind everything.

Stay curious.
